
Title : De Profundis <br>
Author : Christina Rossetti <br><br>

Oh why is heaven built so <mark3>far,</mark3><br>
Oh why is earth set so <mark3>remote?</mark3><br>
I cannot reach the nearest <mark3>star</mark3><br>
That hangs <mark3>afloat.</mark3><br>
<br>
I would not care to reach the <mark3>moon,</mark3><br>
One round monotonous of <mark3>change;</mark3><br>
Yet even she repeats her <mark3>tune</mark3><br>
Beyond my <mark3>range.</mark3><br>
<br>
I never watch the scatter'd <mark4>fire</mark4><br>
Of stars, or sun's far-trailing <mark4>train,</mark4><br>
But all my heart is one <mark3>desire,</mark3><br>
And all in <mark3>vain:</mark3><br>
<br>
For I am bound with fleshly <mark3>bands,</mark3><br>
Joy, beauty, lie beyond my <mark3>scope;</mark3><br>
I strain my heart, I stretch my <mark3>hands,</mark3><br>
And catch at <mark3>hope.</mark3><br>
