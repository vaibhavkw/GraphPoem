
Title : Upon Parson Beanes <br>
Author : Robert Herrick <br><br>

Old Parson Beanes hunts six days of the <mark3>week,</mark3><br>
And on the seventh, he has his notes to <mark3>seek.</mark3><br>
Six days he hollows so much breath <mark3>away</mark3><br>
That on the seventh he can nor preach or <mark3>pray.</mark3><br>
