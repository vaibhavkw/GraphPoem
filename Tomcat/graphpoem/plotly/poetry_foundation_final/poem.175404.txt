
Title : Huswifery <br>
Author : Edward Taylor <br><br>

Make me, O Lord, thy Spining Wheele compleate.<br>
Thy Holy Worde my Distaff make for <mark3>mee.</mark3><br>
Make mine Affections thy Swift Flyers neate<br>
And make my Soule thy holy Spoole to <mark3>bee.</mark3><br>
       My Conversation make to be thy Reele<br>
       And reele the yarn thereon spun of thy Wheele.<br>
<br>
Make me thy Loome <mark4>then</mark4>, knit therein this <mark4>Twine:</mark4><br>
       And make thy Holy Spirit, Lord, winde quills:<br>
Then weave the Web thyselfe. The yarn is <mark3>fine.</mark3><br>
       Thine Ordinances make my Fulling Mills.<br>
Then dy the same in Heavenly Colours <mark4>Choice,</mark4><br>
All pinkt with Varnisht Flowers of <mark4>Paradise.</mark4><br>
<br>
Then cloath therewith mine Understanding, <mark3>Will,</mark3><br>
Affections, Judgment, Conscience, <mark4>Memory</mark4><br>
My Words, and Actions, that their shine may <mark3>fill</mark3><br>
       My wayes with glory and thee glorify.<br>
Then mine apparell shall display before <mark3>yee</mark3><br>
That I am Cloathd in Holy robes for <mark4>glory.</mark4><br>
