
Title : To Lucasta, Going to the Wars <br>
Author : Richard Lovelace <br><br>

Tell me not (Sweet) I am <mark3>unkind,</mark3><br>
         That from the nunnery <br>
Of thy chaste breast and quiet <mark3>mind</mark3><br>
         To war and arms I fly. <br>
<br>
True, a new mistress now I <mark3>chase,</mark3><br>
The first foe in the <mark4>field;</mark4><br>
And with a stronger faith <mark3>embrace</mark3><br>
A sword, a horse, a <mark3>shield.</mark3><br>
<br>
Yet this inconstancy is <mark3>such</mark3><br>
As you too shall <mark3>adore;</mark3><br>
I could not love thee (Dear) so <mark3>much,</mark3><br>
Lov'd I not Honour <mark3>more.</mark3><br>
