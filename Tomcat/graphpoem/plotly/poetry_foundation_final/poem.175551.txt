
Title : VI Mon. August [1742] hath xxxi days. <br>
Author : Benjamin Franklin <br><br>

The Busy-Man's Picture<br>
BUSINESS, thou Plague and Pleasure of my <mark3>Life,</mark3><br>
Thou charming Mistress, thou vexatious <mark3>Wife;</mark3><br>
Thou Enemy, thou Friend, to Joy, to <mark4>Grief,</mark4><br>
Thou bring'st me all, and bring'st me no <mark3>Relief,</mark3><br>
Thou bitter, sweet, thou <mark4>pleasing</mark4>, teazing <mark4>Thing,</mark4><br>
Thou Bee, that with thy Honey wears a <mark3>Sting;</mark3><br>
Some Respite, prithee do, yet do not <mark3>give,</mark3><br>
I cannot with thee, nor without thee <mark3>live.</mark3><br>
