
Title : Mediocrity in Love Rejected <br>
Author : Thomas Carew <br><br>

Give me more love or more <mark4>disdain;</mark4><br>
The torrid, or the frozen <mark4>zone,</mark4><br>
Bring equal ease unto my <mark4>pain;</mark4><br>
The temperate affords me <mark4>none;</mark4><br>
Either extreme, of love, or <mark3>hate,</mark3><br>
Is sweeter than a calm <mark3>estate.</mark3><br>
<br>
Give me a storm; if it be <mark4>love,</mark4><br>
Like Danae in that golden <mark4>show'r</mark4><br>
I swim in pleasure; if it <mark4>prove</mark4><br>
         Disdain, that torrent will devour<br>
My vulture-hopes; and he's <mark4>possess'd</mark4><br>
Of heaven, that's but from hell releas'd.<br>
<br>
Then crown my joys, or cure my <mark1>pain;</mark1><br>
Give me more love, or more <mark1>disdain.</mark1><br>
