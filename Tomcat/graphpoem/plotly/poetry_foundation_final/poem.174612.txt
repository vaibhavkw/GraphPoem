O sorrow, then can sorrow <mark3>wane?</mark3><br>
O grief, can grief be changed to <mark3>less?</mark3><br>
<br>
O last regret, regret can <mark3>die!</mark3><br>
Noimixt with all this mystic <mark3>frame,</mark3><br>
Her deep relations are the <mark3>same,</mark3><br>
