
Title : Music when Soft Voices Die (To --) <br>
Author : Percy Bysshe Shelley <br><br>

   Music, when soft voices die,<br>
Vibrates in the memory?<br>
Odours, when sweet violets <mark3>sicken,</mark3><br>
Live within the sense they <mark3>quicken.</mark3><br>
<br>
Rose leaves, when the rose is <mark3>dead,</mark3><br>
Are heaped for the beloved's <mark3>bed;</mark3><br>
And so thy thoughts, when thou art <mark4>gone,</mark4><br>
Love itself shall slumber <mark3>on.</mark3><br>
