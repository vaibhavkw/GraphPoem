
Title : Little Elegy <br>
Author : Elinor Wylie <br><br>

Withouten you <br>
No rose can grow; <br>
No leaf be <mark3>green</mark3><br>
If never <mark3>seen</mark3><br>
Your sweetest <mark3>face;</mark3><br>
No bird have <mark3>grace</mark3><br>
Or power to <mark4>sing;</mark4><br>
Or <mark4>anything</mark4><br>
Be kind, or <mark4>fair,</mark4><br>
And you <mark4>nowhere.</mark4><br>
