
Title : A Poem for Speculative Hipsters <br>
Author : Amiri Baraka <br><br>

He had got, <mark4>finally,</mark4><br>
to the forest <br>
of motives. There were no <br>
owls, or hunters. No Connie Chatterleys   <br>
resting <mark4>beautifully</mark4><br>
on their backs, having <mark4>casually</mark4><br>
brought socialism <br>
to England.   <br>
                  Only ideas, <br>
and their opposites. <br>
<br>
he was <mark4>really</mark4><br>
